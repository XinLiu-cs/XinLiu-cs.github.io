# Xin Liu (刘鑫)

* I am a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science & Engineering at the University of Michigan and a member of the [LAUNCH](https://launch.eecs.umich.edu/home) lab, advised by Prof. [Lu Wang](https://web.eecs.umich.edu/~wangluxy/index.html). My research builds **trustworthy and efficient large language models** — spanning factuality, confidence calibration, reasoning and efficient inference, long-form evaluation, and knowledge distillation / post-training, and, most recently, interpretability and safety. I enjoy building large-scale training and evaluation pipelines (for 1B–70B models) and turning research into reliable, deployable model behavior. Previously, I received my master's and bachelor's degrees at Xiamen University, where I was advised by Prof. [Jinsong Su](https://cdmc.xmu.edu.cn/info/1010/1054.htm).
